Our Purpose

On this site you get to explore the world of procedural art, and create some awesome stuff to show your friends! Our goal is to inspire people to enhance their creativity with technology.

What is procedural art?

( Also called Generative Art or Dynamic Drawing )

In this age of technology, there are more tools than ever before to help us make super-cool art!

Procedural art is an amazing way to create complex looking designs and images using computers. These computer programs can generate mind-blowing images without too much effort.

Why should you learn more about procedural art?

If you're an artist in today's world, then using technology to enhance your art is pretty typical. There are certain advantages that using computers gives to artists that would not be possible any other way.
